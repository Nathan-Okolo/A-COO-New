Visa Applications Requirements

Introduction

It is important to note that applying for a visa to study in the UK from Nigeria is now handled by VFS Global instead of TLS. This change took effect on November 19, 2024. The official site for applying for a UK Visa is the official UK Government Website, www.gov.uk. Subsequent agencies manage the biometrics and documentation. As of today, VTS Global is the official partner of the UK government in Nigeria for visa application and processing.

Basic Requirements for a Student Visa

For a student, there are three basic requirements for a visa application and a prospective successful outcome. These are:

  1. Financial Requirements
  2. Document Requirements
  3. Identification Requirements
  4. Medical/Tuberculosis Test

Drivers Licence Although not essential, it is required if one needs to drive or use an automobile while abroad. This allows you to drive without a UK driving licence for one year from your arrival in the UK. It expires one year after your arrival, and if you are found driving, it is a criminal offence punishable by law and court summons.

4. Medical/Tuberculosis Test

It is important to ensure that the TB test is conducted at an IOM organization. This test should be completed well in advance to accommodate any potential delays, such as a sputum test, which can take up to 8 weeks (2 months) for results. Waiting until one or two weeks before applying for a visa to conduct the TB test can jeopardize a student's overall preparation and travel plans. It may be too late if the 8-week waiting period is not factored in. Please remember that the TB test result and certificate are prerequisites for the visa application.

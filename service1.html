Admissions and International Studies

Do you want to study in Nigeria or the UK (Campus and distance learning) for a foundation, Undergraduate, Premasters, Masters, or PhD? Do you want your statement of purpose (SOP), personal statement, proposal for PhD, CV, and transcript to be evaluated for eligibility for admission? Welcome.

We pride ourselves on being expert assistants for international admissions in the UK, with 95% success and higher chances of scholarships and discounts. We partner with other agencies and universities to give you a 100% FREE admission service, from application to issuance of CAS. We could also help you apply for a visa. With us, there is always a university for anyone, and there is always a course for everyone.

We perform the following tasks under here

i. Undergraduate admissions

We assist and process undergraduate admissions to and for various schools in Nigeria and abroad, our undergraduate admissions come services come with professional counselling and advisory, tutorials and preparation classes

ii. Post graduate admission

We have the most excellent assistants for postgraduate admissions for taught and research programs in Nigeria and abroad including UK and US, we provide services from document reviews, school search, course choice, application and all required processes to issuance of admission letter and subsequently CAS.
